title: "OSHA 1910.147 LOTO procedure builder and reviewer skill"
description: "Generate OSHA 1910.147-compliant lockout/tagout procedures with energy source identification, device assignments, and isolation sequences."
---

The `loto-procedure-builder` produces a complete lockout/tagout procedure workbook compliant with OSHA 1910.147 — the Control of Hazardous Energy standard. It identifies all energy sources (electrical, pneumatic, hydraulic, stored/potential, gravity), assigns LOTO devices to each, and generates a step-by-step procedure for authorized employees. It is the second operational skill in the chain, consuming the robot SOP and feeding directly into the operator training matrix.

<Note>
**Chain position:** Consumes `robot-sop-builder` output. Feeds into `operator-training-matrix-builder`. Terminal if no training matrix is needed.
</Note>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Builder">
    **Trigger phrase:**
    ```text
    Write a LOTO procedure for [machine/robot cell description]
    ```

    **Workbook output — tabs:**

    | Tab | Content |
    |-----|---------|
    | Energy Sources | Electrical panels, pneumatic supplies, hydraulic circuits, gravity loads, stored energy (springs, capacitors) |
    | LOTO Devices | Hasp, padlock, lockout tag, valve lockout, plug lockout — assigned per energy source |
    | Procedure Steps | Notify → Shutdown → Isolation → Apply LOTO → Release stored energy → Verify de-energization |
    | Authorized Employees | Named list with LOTO device assignments |
    | Restoration Steps | Verify clear → Remove LOTO → Notify → Re-energize |

    **OSHA 1910.147 procedural sequence:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Notify affected employees">
        Inform all employees in the area that the machine will be shut down and locked out.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Normal shutdown">
        Follow the robot SOP shutdown sequence to bring the machine to a complete stop.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Energy isolation">
        Operate all isolating devices to disconnect every identified energy source.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Apply LOTO devices">
        Each authorized employee applies their personal padlock and tag to every isolating device.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Release stored energy">
        Bleed pneumatic lines, discharge capacitors, block gravity loads, release spring tension.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify de-energization">
        Attempt to start the machine (jog button test) and verify zero energy with a meter.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Reviewer">
    The `loto-procedure-checklist-reviewer` runs a confirmation-measures checklist against the LOTO procedure workbook and produces a visual dashboard.

    **Dashboard components:**
    - KPI tiles: energy source count, device assignment completeness %, procedural step count
    - Compliance bar: OSHA 1910.147 clause coverage
    - Findings table: gaps with recommended actions (e.g., missing stored energy release step)

    **Checks performed:**
    - All energy types identified (electrical, pneumatic, hydraulic, stored, gravity)
    - Every energy source has a named isolating device and LOTO device assigned
    - Six-step OSHA procedure present and in correct order
    - Stored energy release step explicitly included
    - Verification step (attempt to start + meter check) present
    - Authorized employee list populated
    - Restoration procedure included
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
