---
title: "Robot operator training matrix builder and reviewer"
description: "Generate per-role qualification matrices with competency areas, training requirements, assessment methods, and re-qualification intervals for robot cell staff."
---

The `operator-training-matrix-builder` produces a per-role qualification matrix covering all personnel who interact with a robot cell — operators, technicians, programmers, maintenance staff, and supervisors. For each role it defines required competency areas, training delivery method, assessment approach, and re-qualification interval. It is the terminal skill in the operational lane, consuming the robot SOP and LOTO procedure as upstream inputs.

<Note>
**Chain position:** Consumes `robot-sop-builder` and `loto-procedure-builder` outputs. Terminal node in the operational lane.
</Note>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Builder">
    **Trigger phrase:**
    ```text
    Build an operator training matrix for [robot cell/facility description]
    ```

    **Workbook output — tabs:**

    | Tab | Content |
    |-----|---------|
    | Role Definitions | Role name, scope, authority level, supervision requirements |
    | Competency Matrix | Role × competency area grid with required proficiency level (Awareness / Competent / Expert) |
    | Training Plan | Training topic, delivery method (classroom/OJT/CBT), duration, prerequisite |
    | Assessment Methods | Written test, practical demonstration, observed performance, sign-off |
    | Qualification Records | Template for tracking individual completion dates and assessor signatures |
    | Re-qualification Schedule | Interval per role (e.g., annual for operators, biennial for programmers) |

    **Roles covered:**

    | Role | Typical competency focus |
    |------|--------------------------|
    | Operator | Safe startup/shutdown, fault response, E-stop use, no-go zone awareness |
    | Maintenance technician | LOTO, mechanical/electrical fault diagnosis, safeguard bypass procedures |
    | Robot programmer | Teaching pendant use, speed limits, collaborative mode configuration |
    | Supervisor | Emergency procedures, incident reporting, override authority |
    | Safety officer | Risk assessment review, compliance audit, corrective action |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Reviewer">
    The `operator-training-matrix-checklist-reviewer` checks the training matrix for completeness against the cell's SOP and LOTO procedure.

    **Dashboard components:**
    - KPI tiles: role count, competency area count, re-qualification interval compliance
    - Compliance bar: ISO 10218-2 training clause coverage
    - Findings table: missing competency areas or roles with recommended actions

    **Checks performed:**
    - All roles identified that appear in the SOP and LOTO procedure
    - LOTO competency included for maintenance and authorized employee roles
    - E-stop and emergency response competency present for all roles
    - Assessment method defined for every competency area
    - Re-qualification intervals specified and justified
    - Qualification record template included
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
